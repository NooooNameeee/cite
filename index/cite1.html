Information

McLaren is a British manufacturer of sports and racing cars founded in 1963 by Bruce McLaren. The company is renowned for its innovative technologies and high standards of quality. Initially focused on Formula 1 racing, McLaren achieved significant success in the sport, winning numerous championships. In the 1990s, the company began producing road-going supercars, with the iconic McLaren F1 being the first. Today, McLaren continues to produce exclusive vehicles such as the 720S and Senna, which combine cutting-edge technology with exceptional performance. The company's headquarters are located in Woking, UK, where the McLaren Technology Centre is also situated.
